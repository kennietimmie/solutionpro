Career Acceleration Coaching Program

Course Objective: This online course uses the coaching model on how to achieve your desired career progression. If you are unhappy due to lack of growth or promotion or you no longer find your work interesting and you are seeking a change as quickly as possible. Then this course is for you.

Module 1

CLARITY IN YOUR CAREER: This module provides step by step coaching programs to help you in gaining clarity and how you can find fulfillment in your career.
Lesson 1: Starting your journey to self-discovery & self – awareness
Lesson 2: Understand your personality
Lesson 3: Career Identify. Finding Your Career Purpose
Lesson 4: What am I cut out for? How do I know the best career fit for me?
Lesson 5: My Career direction/map: where am I headed? Self-reflection series

Module 2

THE RIGHT POSITION FOR YOUR CAREER
Lesson 1: Assessing your current competencies and skills
Lesson 2: How to discover the values in your abilities
Lesson 3: How to find the solutions you have the competences to solve
Lesson 4: How to find your career fit

Module 3

STRATEGY FOR CAREER TRANSFORMATION
Lesson 1: Define your end goal
Lesson 2: How to build your compelling story
Lesson 3:How to build your career networks
Lesson 4: Career transformational journey guide/journal
